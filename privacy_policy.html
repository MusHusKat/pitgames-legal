Privacy Policy

Snake by Pit Games  ·  Last updated: February 2026

Pit Games ("we", "us", or "our") built Snake as a free mobile game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The current version of Snake does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data (player name, score, level, selected theme, selected skin) is stored locally on your device only using Android's shared preferences storage. This data never leaves your device.

Device Permissions

The app requests the following permission:

  • Internet — declared for future features (leaderboards, cloud save). The current version does not make any network requests.

Third-Party Services

The current version of Snake does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. We plan to add these in a future update, at which point this policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

Snake is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as new features are added to the app. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
